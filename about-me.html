About Me

Hi there! I am Nooria Amiri a girl with big dreams, a curious heart, and a deep love for technology. I come from Maidan Wardak, Afghanistan, but my story begins in the bustling city of Kabul, where I was born, raised, and inspired.

Growing up surrounded by challenges made me stronger, more determined, and more passionate about making a difference. I believe that every line of code can create change not just on a screen, but in the real world.
That is why I have set my heart on becoming a software engineer.

To me, technology is more than wires and code it is a language of hope. It is a way to solve problems, connect people, and dream bigger than borders. I want to use what I learn to give back to my community, to empower girls like me.
And to prove that nothing is impossible when you believe in yourself.
